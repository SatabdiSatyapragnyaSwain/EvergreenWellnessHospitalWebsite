Skin & Cosmetology

Skin and cosmetology focus on diagnosing and treating skin conditions, enhancing appearance, and promoting overall skin health. This specialty covers a range of services, from managing acne, eczema, and pigmentation to advanced cosmetic procedures like chemical peels, laser treatments, and anti-aging therapies. With the integration of dermatology and cosmetology, it ensures both medical and aesthetic skin care.
