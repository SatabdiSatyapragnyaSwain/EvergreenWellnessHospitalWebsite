Psychiatrist

Psychiatry is a medical specialty dedicated to diagnosing, treating, and preventing mental, emotional, and behavioral disorders. Psychiatrists are trained to understand the complex relationship between the mind and body and provide holistic care for conditions like anxiety, depression, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, and more. Treatment methods include therapy, counseling, and medication management.
