Gastroenterology Department

Gastroenterology is the medical specialty focused on the diagnosis and treatment of conditions related to the digestive system, including the esophagus, stomach, intestines, liver, pancreas, and gallbladder. It addresses issues such as acid reflux, irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), liver diseases, and gastrointestinal cancers. Advanced endoscopic procedures are often used for both diagnosis and treatment.
