Cosmetic & Reconstructive

Cosmetic and reconstructive surgery focuses on enhancing physical appearance and restoring functionality after trauma, illness, or congenital conditions. Cosmetic surgery aims to improve aesthetics and self-confidence through procedures like facelifts, liposuction, and rhinoplasty. Reconstructive surgery addresses medical conditions such as burns, deformities, and post-surgical recovery, including breast reconstruction or scar revision.
