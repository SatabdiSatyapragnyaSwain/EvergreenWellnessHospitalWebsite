Neurosurgery

Neurosurgery is a specialized medical field dedicated to diagnosing and surgically treating conditions related to the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system. It addresses issues such as brain tumors, spinal disc problems, stroke, epilepsy, and nerve injuries. Using advanced techniques like minimally invasive surgery and robotics, neurosurgery ensures precision and faster recovery, significantly improving patients' quality of life.
