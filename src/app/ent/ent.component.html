ENT (Ear, Nose, and Throat) Surgery

ENT

ENT surgery focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of conditions related to the ear, nose, and throat. ENT specialists, also known as Otolaryngologists, manage a wide range of issues including hearing loss, sinus problems, voice disorders, and conditions affecting the throat and airway. They also treat issues such as nasal blockages, ear infections, and tonsillitis.

Common ENT Procedures:

Tonsillectomy: Removal of the tonsils to treat recurring infections.

Sinus Surgery: A procedure to clear blocked sinuses and treat chronic sinus infections.

Ear Surgery: Treatment of ear conditions like ear infections, hearing loss, and eardrum repair.

Adenoidectomy: Removal of the adenoids, often due to infection or breathing issues.

Throat Surgery: Treatment of conditions affecting the voice box, such as tumors or chronic conditions like sleep apnea.
