Orthopedics & Trauma

Orthopedics focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of conditions affecting the bones, joints, muscles, ligaments, and tendons. Trauma care in orthopedics involves the management of fractures, dislocations, and injuries caused by accidents or other emergencies. Specialists in orthopedics and trauma help patients regain mobility, relieve pain, and restore functionality through advanced treatments, surgical interventions, and rehabilitation.
