Urology Department

Urology is the branch of medicine focused on the diagnosis, treatment, and management of conditions affecting the urinary tract in both men and women, as well as the male reproductive system. It encompasses a wide range of issues, including urinary infections, kidney stones, bladder problems, prostate disorders, and male infertility. Advanced procedures like minimally invasive surgeries and endoscopic treatments are commonly used in urology.
