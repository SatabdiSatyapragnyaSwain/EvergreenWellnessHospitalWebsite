Ophthalmology

Ophthalmology is the branch of medicine that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of eye and vision-related disorders. Ophthalmologists manage a wide range of conditions, including cataracts, glaucoma, retinal diseases, refractive errors, and eye injuries. Advanced techniques like laser surgeries and corrective procedures are integral to modern ophthalmology.
