Gynaecology & Obstetrics

Gynaecology focuses on the health of the female reproductive system, addressing conditions like menstrual irregularities, infertility, and hormonal disorders. Obstetrics deals with pregnancy care, childbirth, and postnatal support, ensuring the well-being of both mother and baby. Together, these specialties cover comprehensive women's health, from adolescence to menopause and beyond.
