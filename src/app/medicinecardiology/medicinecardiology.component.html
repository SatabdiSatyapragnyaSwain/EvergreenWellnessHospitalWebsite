Medicine & Cardiology

Medicine and Cardiology department focuses on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of various medical and heart-related conditions. General medicine covers a broad spectrum of diseases affecting different organ systems, while cardiology specializes in disorders of the heart and circulatory system, such as hypertension, coronary artery disease, arrhythmias, and heart failure. Advanced diagnostic tools and treatments, including non-invasive tests and interventional cardiology procedures, are used to ensure comprehensive care.
