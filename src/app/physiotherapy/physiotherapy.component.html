Physiotherapy Department

Physiotherapy focuses on restoring and maintaining physical function and mobility through tailored therapeutic exercises, manual therapy, and advanced modalities. It is vital for rehabilitation after injuries, surgeries, or chronic conditions affecting the musculoskeletal, neurological, or cardiovascular systems. Physiotherapy also aids in pain management and enhances overall physical wellness.

Why Choose Evergreen Wellness Hospital for Physiotherapy?

Expert Physiotherapists: A team of highly trained and experienced professionals dedicated to personalized care.

Comprehensive Rehabilitation Programs: Individualized plans designed for post-surgical recovery, injury management, and chronic pain relief.

Modern Equipment: Advanced technology and techniques, including electrotherapy, ultrasound therapy, and hydrotherapy, for effective treatment.

Holistic Approach: Focus on both physical recovery and emotional well-being to ensure a balanced and sustained recovery.

Collaborative Care: Close coordination with other medical specialists for integrated treatment plans.

Patient-Centric Environment: Friendly and supportive care for patients of all ages and conditions.
